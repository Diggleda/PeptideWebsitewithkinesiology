How to use Patient Links

1. Open Patient Links in your physician dashboard, then choose Create a link.
2. Product Brochure creates a view-only product information page with no pricing, cart, or checkout.
3. Delegate creates a patient session that can build an order proposal for physician review.
4. Use Manage your links to preview, copy, track, revoke, and review proposal activity.
