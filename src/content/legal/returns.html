Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Eligibility

All sales are final unless the product arrives damaged, incorrect, or incomplete (e.g., missing items). If you believe there is an issue with your order, contact us promptly so we can review and help.

2. Reporting window

Requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery. Claims submitted after this window may be denied.

3. Return authorization

Do not ship products back without written authorization. Unauthorized returns may be refused or handled case-by-case, and a restocking fee of up to $100 may be deducted from any customer credit.

4. Evidence and packaging

When submitting a request, please include your order number and a clear description of the issue. For damaged shipments, include photos of the outer box, inner packaging, and the product label(s). Please keep all original packaging until the issue is resolved.

5. Refunds and resolutions

If approved, refunds are issued to the original form of payment. Depending on the circumstance, we may also offer a replacement shipment or store credit. Refund processing times vary by payment provider and financial institution.

How to request: Email support@peppro.net and include your order number, the reason for the request, and (if applicable) photos of damage.
