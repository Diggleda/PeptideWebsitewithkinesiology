Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2026
Version: 2026.05.23

This Returns & Refunds Policy applies to orders placed through TrufusionLabs websites, portals, physician-directed research workflows, and authorized-recipient links. It is part of the Terms of Service.

1. Final sale baseline

Because TrufusionLabs products are research-use-only materials that may be subject to integrity, chain-of-custody, verification, and handling considerations, all sales are final unless we approve a return, refund, replacement, or credit in writing.

2. Eligible issues

We will review requests involving damaged shipments, incorrect products, missing items, incomplete orders, fulfillment errors, duplicate charges, or other issues we determine are eligible. Buyer remorse, ordering mistakes, unused products, expired eligibility, address errors, recipient unavailability, or unauthorized use generally do not qualify.

3. Reporting window

Requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery or pickup availability. Claims submitted after this window may be denied.

4. Return authorization required

Do not ship products back without written authorization from TrufusionLabs. Unauthorized returns may be refused, discarded, or handled case by case, and a restocking, review, or reshipment fee may be deducted from any approved credit or refund.

5. Evidence and packaging

When submitting a request, include your order number, a clear description of the issue, and relevant photos. For damaged, incorrect, or incomplete shipments, include photos of the outer box, inner packaging, packing materials, product label, and affected item. Keep all original packaging until the issue is resolved.

6. Approved resolutions

If a request is approved, TrufusionLabs may issue a refund to the original payment method, provide a replacement shipment, apply account credit, correct the order, or take another reasonable action. Refund timing depends on the payment provider and financial institution.

7. How to request review

Email support@trufusionlabs.com with your order number, reason for the request, and supporting photos or details.
